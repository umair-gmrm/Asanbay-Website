# 19-share-article-linkedin-low

**As a** website visitor (logged in or not)  
**I want to** share an article on LinkedIn  
**So that** I can share professional content with my network

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- LinkedIn share button is visible on article page
- Clicking button opens LinkedIn share dialog
- Article metadata is properly formatted for LinkedIn
- User can add personal message before sharing
- Button is mobile responsive
- **No login required** - anyone can share articles
