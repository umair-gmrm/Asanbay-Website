# 17-share-article-facebook-medium

**As a** website visitor (logged in or not)  
**I want to** share an article on Facebook  
**So that** I can spread awareness about the society's content

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Facebook share button is visible on article page
- Clicking button opens Facebook share dialog
- Article title, description, and URL are pre-populated
- User can post to their Facebook timeline
- Share count is tracked (optional)
- Button is mobile responsive
- **No login required** - anyone can share articles
